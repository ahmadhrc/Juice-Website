Our Story

Founded in 2015, FreshPress began with a simple mission: to make healthy eating delicious and accessible to everyone.

Our founders, a nutritionist and a chef, combined their expertise to create a menu that's as nutritious as it is delicious. Every recipe is crafted to maximize health benefits without compromising on taste.

We source our ingredients from local organic farms whenever possible, and we're committed to sustainable practices in every aspect of our business.
